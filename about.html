About The Streak Lab

Simple, private 30-day habit experiments. No apps, no accounts, no guilt — just your own observable data.

Why We Built This

Most habit trackers want your email, your data, your subscription. We wanted the opposite: zero friction, maximum privacy, real experimentation.

Inspired by "don't break the chain" calendars, Atomic Habits-style resets, and the idea that small, time-boxed tests beat vague "lifestyle changes."

What We Are (and Aren't)

  • We are: A free collection of browser-based streak trackers
  • We are: 100% local — your progress never leaves your device
  • We are: Focused on curiosity and observation, not perfection
  • We aren't: A productivity app with notifications or gamification
  • We aren't: Medical, financial, or psychological advice
  • We aren't: Collecting any data about you

How It Started

The original Streak Lab was an NBA player prop streak dashboard. We loved the "heat index" concept — tracking hot/cold runs with clear data.

We realized the same mechanics (streaks, regression, no-shame resets) could apply to personal habits. So we rebuilt it from the ground up — privacy-first, evergreen, and guilt-free.

Our Philosophy

  • 30 days is long enough to see change, short enough to finish.
  • Minimum viable wins beat impossible standards.
  • Data without judgment — misses are information, not failure.
  • Privacy is non-negotiable in 2026.
