Why Track NBA Player Performance Streaks?

In NBA prop betting, historical averages often hide the reality of a player's current form. A player averaging 20 points per game might actually be on a cold streak, while a bench player might be seeing increased usage due to injuries. The Streak Lab Heat Index reveals the current momentum of 98+ key athletes.

How to Use Our NBA Heat Map

Our dashboard allows you to toggle "Sharp Mode," which instantly filters the league to show only athletes with an 80% or higher hit rate over their last 10 games. This is essential for identifying high-probability over/under picks and building winning parlays.

Data Methodology & Reliability

All data is processed using Python via the nba_api, ensuring the statistics are pulled from official league sources. We update the NBA Prop Trends daily at 10:00 AM EST to reflect the previous night's results. By analyzing the frequency of "hits" rather than just raw averages, we provide a clearer picture of player consistency.
